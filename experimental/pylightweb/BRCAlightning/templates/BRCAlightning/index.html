Welcome to BRCA-Lightning!

This site is a work in progress and is open-source. Please contact us here with questions, unexpected behavior reports, and feature requests.

Our goals include enabling fast analysis of population genetic data as part of the GA4GH BRCA Challenge. We currently use whole genome sequenced (WGS) data (covering chr13p11.1 to chr13q34 and all of chr17) obtained from the Personal Genome Project.

The 174 WGS sequences available here have been converted losslessly to Compressed Genome Format (CGF), a developmental format enabling simple lift-over, annotation, visualization, and fast queries and analysis of human genomes.
