    Welcome to Lightning!

    This site is a work in progress and is open-source

    Our goals include enabling fast analysis of population genetic data as part of the GA4GH BRCA Challenge. We currently use whole genome sequence data (covering chr13q13.1 and chr17q21.31 only) obtained from the Personal Genome Project. To view the entire genomes of a smaller portion of the population, visit the other Lightning page. These sequences have been converted losslessly to Compressed Genome Format, a developmental format enabling simple lift-over, annotation, visualization, and fast analysis of human genomes.

    • To view the Tile Library, visit the Tile Library Statistics tab.
    • To search for UCSC genes and corresponding Gene Review Articles, visit the Known Genes Tab.

    Our team has implemented Beacon for the Personal Genome Project Data. Visit Beacon to experiment with our preliminary implementation.

    Known Genes data downloaded from the UCSC Human Genome Browser (hg19). Thanks to Hiram Clawson, Brooke Rhead, Pauline Fujita, Ann Zweig, Katrina Learned, Donna Karolchik and Robert Kuhn.
