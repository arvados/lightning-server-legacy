    Welcome to Lightning!

    This site is a work in progress and is open-source

    Our goals include enabling fast analysis of population genetic data. We currently use whole genome sequence data obtained from the Personal Genome Project. These sequences have been converted losslessly to Compressed Genome Format, a developmental format enabling simple lift-over, annotation, visualization, and fast analysis of human genomes.

    • To view the population of 154 humans, search for genes, and view genes with Gene Review articles, visit the Population tab (an explanation of the process of visualization will be coming soon).
    • To view individual human data, visit the Individuals Tab.

    Our team is working on implementing Beacon for the Personal Genome Project Data. Visit Beacon to experiment with our preliminary implementation.
